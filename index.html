The Making of my Favourite dish!

My favourite food is concortion rice and beans. I like it so much because it is very easy to prepare and delicious when adequately prepared with the right spices

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Some clean water
  1. Put some clean water in a cooking pot and add a desired quantity of bean seed in it 😋
  2. Allow the beans to cook for a while to soften the seeds [Rice cooked faster than bean seeds] 😋
  3. Add a desired quantity of rice in the pot 😋
  4. Add the pepper or other spices and vegetable oil in it, wait till the food is done 😋
  5. Add some salt to taste 😋
  6. When the dish is well done, I serve it with soft drink or water 😋 😋

See some pictures of how the dish looks like after preparation.😋 you will love it!

The end result is a sumptuous dish with a sweet smelling savor of nutritious food. You need to try it and feel the taste of life!
